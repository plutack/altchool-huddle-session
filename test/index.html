Biography

Steve Paul Jobs(February 24, 1955 - October 5, 2011) was an American enterpreneur, industrial Designer, business magnate. He is widely recognized as a pioneer ; and the founder , chairman and CEO of NeXT. He is widely recognized as a pioneer of personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co founder Steve Wozniak
